Mixed-Nut-Milk Panna Cotta

Brutti Ma Buoni

    Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons unflavored powdered gelatin
  • 3 cups mixed nut milk
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 vanilla bean, split and seeds scraped
  • salt and Vegetable oil
  • Crushed toasted hazelnuts
  • 1/4 cup strawberry jam whisked with 1 tablespoon water

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups hazelnuts
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • salt
  • 1 large egg white
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Steps

Steps-1
First mix the sprinkle the gelatin over 1/2 cup of the nut milk and wait for the softened it. In other pan mix the remaining 2 1/2 cups of nut milk with the sugar, honey, vanilla seeds and salt. Next heat the pan whisks it the softened.
Steps-2
brush six 1/2-cup ramekins with oil and set them on a baking sheet and fill the panna cotta mixture and refrigerate it about 2 hours.
Steps-3
After 2 hours in refrigerator, panna cotta and invert onto a plate and garnish panna cotta with crushed hazelnuts and serve with the jam.

Steps

Steps-1
Spread the hazelnuts on a baking sheet and toast it about 10 minutes. After that transfer the hazelnuts to bowl and remove the hazelnuts skin.
Steps-2
Mix the hazelnuts with the confectioners' sugar and salt until finely chopped. After that beat the egg and add to the mixture with vanilla.
Steps-3
After that spoon tablespoon-size mounds of the hazelnut dough onto the prepared baking sheet 1 inch apart.
Steps-4
Bake the cookies in oven for about 15 minutes.
