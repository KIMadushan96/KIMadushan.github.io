Spaghetti

Pasta

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1 cup of Basil leaves & tomato

    Ingredients

  • 1 pounds pasta
  • 4 tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 boiled egg
  • 8 ounces Robiola Rocchetta cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup tomato sauce & cup of basil leaves

Steps

Steps-1
Cook the spaghetti in a boiling salted water.
Steps-2
Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking water and add the cheese.
Steps-3
Add the tomato, black paper and half cup of basil leaves. After that mix it all using the spoon.
Steps-4
Transfer the spaghetti to a plate and garnishing with tomato, Basil leaves.
